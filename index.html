How It Works

From browse to
your door — 4 steps.

Browse & Pick

Open Flow, see designers near you sorted by distance. Each listing shows real photos and a live delivery window. Tap a piece you love.

Pay Into Escrow

You pay — but your money goes into a protected vault, not to the designer. Not yet. It waits until you're satisfied.

Courier Arrives

A vetted Flow courier picks it up from the studio and comes to your gate. Live tracking the whole way. Under 60 minutes.

Try On, Then Decide

Courier waits. You try it on. Love it? Tap "It's a fit" — payment releases. Doesn't work? Hand it back. Full refund, no stories.
